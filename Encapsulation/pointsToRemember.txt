> In java, Encapsulation is a mechanism od wrappinf the data(variables) and code acting on the data(methods) together as a single unit.
> Variables of a class will be hidden from other classes, and can be accesed only through the methods of their current class is called data hiding.
> To achieve encapsulation in java:
    - declare the  variable of a class as private
    - provide public setter and getter methods to modify and view the variables values
